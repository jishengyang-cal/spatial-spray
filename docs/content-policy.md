# Content Policy Draft

Spatial Spray allows digital AR artwork only. It does not permit or encourage
damage, vandalism, trespass, or modification of real property.

## Not Allowed

- Hate, harassment, threats, or targeted abuse.
- Sexual content in public discovery surfaces.
- Private information, doxxing, or surveillance.
- Spam, scams, impersonation, or malicious links.
- Content placed at sensitive or prohibited locations.
- Instructions or encouragement to damage real-world property.

## Location Restrictions

Production must support location exclusion zones for sensitive places, owner
requests, private-property complaints, schools, hospitals, government
buildings, residential addresses, and event-specific safety zones.

Owner-only visibility is available for non-public places such as homes and
companies. It reduces public discovery exposure, but it does not authorize
abusive content, trespass, or unsafe placement.

## Moderation Workflow

Users can report a spray piece and block a user. Admins can review reports,
hide or remove pieces, mark content visible, add location denylist entries, and
review audit events. Owners can switch their own pieces between public and
owner-only visibility. Repeated abuse should lead to account restrictions.

## Review Notes

All public product copy should describe spray pieces as digital overlays.
Do not use language that suggests permission to paint or alter physical
surfaces.
